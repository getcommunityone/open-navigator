# 1. WHY IS YOUR RENT SO HIGH?

[HOOK]

Quick question.

Why is rent expensive?

A) Greedy landlords

B) Greedy developers

C) Greedy corporations

D) Local zoning laws you've never heard of

Lock in your answer.

...

The correct answer?

E) All of the above.

Nobody wins.

[MAIN]

Imagine you own land.

You want to build apartments.

Simple.

Except first you need:

- Parking requirements
- Height limits
- Setbacks
- Traffic studies
- Public hearings
- Neighborhood reviews

Now let's hear both sides.

Team Neighborhood:

"We like our community."

"We don't want traffic."

"We don't want overcrowded schools."

Fair.

Team Housing:

"If every neighborhood says no...

where do new residents live?"

Also fair.

Here's the hidden mechanic:

More housing restrictions usually mean fewer homes get built.

Fewer homes.

More demand.

Higher prices.

[ENDING]

Housing isn't a villain story.

It's a tradeoff story.
